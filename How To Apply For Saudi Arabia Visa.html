Visa Application Process for Saudi Arabia

Application Steps

  1. Visit the official Enjazit website to fill out the visa application form.

  2. Upload the required documents and pay the visa fee online.

  3. Submit Documents to the Saudi Embassy or Visa Center

    After completing the online application, submit your documents to the Saudi Embassy in Islamabad or the nearest Saudi Visa Center in Pakistan.

    The Saudi Visa Application Centers (VACs) are located in major cities like Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad.

  4. Attend Biometrics Appointment

    You may need to visit the visa center for biometrics (fingerprints and photo).

  5. Wait for Visa Processing

  6. Collect Your Visa

    Once approved, you will be notified to collect your visa from the embassy or visa center.

Saudi Visa Application Centers in Pakistan

  • Islamabad: Saudi Visa Application Center, Blue Area, Islamabad.
  • Karachi: Saudi Visa Application Center, Clifton, Karachi.
  • Lahore: Saudi Visa Application Center, Gulberg, Lahore.

Important Tips

  • Work Visa: Your employer in Saudi Arabia must initiate the visa process by obtaining a visa authorization from the Saudi Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
  • Hajj/Umrah Visa: These visas are only issued through authorized Hajj/Umrah tour operators approved by the Saudi government.
  • Tourist Visa: Saudi Arabia now offers e-visas for tourists, which can be applied for online.
